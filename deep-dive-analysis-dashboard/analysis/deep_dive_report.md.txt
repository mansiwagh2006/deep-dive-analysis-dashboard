# Deep-Dive Analysis Report

## Objective
To answer complex business questions through deep-dive analysis and an
interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Core KPIs
- Total Revenue = Sum of total_sales
- Total Orders = Distinct count of order_id
- Average Order Value (AOV) = Revenue / Orders
- Total Quantity Sold = Sum of weight_kg

## Deep-Dive Focus
Product Performance Analysis

## Key Insights
1. Some products generate high revenue but sell less quantity.
2. Monthly sales show seasonality.
3. High-volume products do not always produce high revenue.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus promotions on high-revenue products.
- Review pricing for high-volume products.
- Plan inventory for peak months.
